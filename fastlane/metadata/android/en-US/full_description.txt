<p><i>WeatherMaster</i> inspired by the Google Pixel weather app, but with more features</p>
<br>
<p>Features both the Pixel Weather-inspired design and the original Froggy style, with summaries, multiple weather and search providers, personalization options, and more.</p>
<br>
<p><b>Supported weather sources: </b></p>

<ul>
 <li><b>Open Meteo</b></li>
 <li><b>Met Norway</b></li>
 <li><b>NWS</b></li>
 <li><b>SMHI</b></li>
 <li><b>DWD</b></li>
 <li><b>Meteo-France</b></li>
 <li><b>FMI</b></li>
 <li><b>ECCC</b></li>
 <li><b>BMKG</b></li>
 <li><b>China</b></li>
 <li><b>And more to be added</b></li>
</ul>
